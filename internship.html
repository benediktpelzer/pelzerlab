Research Internships

We offer research internships for students at all levels who want to get a first hands-on experience in a translational research environment. Interns will be involved in ongoing lab projects and get exposure to state-of-the-art techniques in molecular biology, epigenomics, and data analysis.

Funding for Internships

Several funding sources are available to support research internships:

  • DAAD RISE — research internships in science and engineering for international students
  • Humboldt Foundation — fellowship opportunities for international research stays in Germany
