Postdoctoral Researchers

Develop your independent research career in our lab.

Postdoc Positions

We welcome applications from motivated postdoctoral researchers interested in B cell lymphoma biology, epigenomics, and translational oncology. We are committed to supporting postdocs in developing their independent research profile and career.

Funding for Postdoctoral Researchers

We actively support applications for independent postdoctoral fellowships including:

  • DFG Emmy Noether Programme — independent research group funding for outstanding early-career researchers
  • Marie Skłodowska-Curie Postdoctoral Fellowship — European fellowship for international mobility and research excellence
  • Humboldt Research Fellowship — prestigious fellowship for international postdocs coming to Germany
  • José Carreras Foundation — research fellowships for leukemia and lymphoma research
  • Mildred Scheel Postdoctoral Fellowship — postdoctoral fellowships from the Deutsche Krebshilfe
  • EMBO Postdoctoral Fellowship — prestigious fellowship for life scientists

How to Apply

If you are interested in joining the lab as a postdoctoral researcher, please send your CV, a brief statement of research interests, and contact details of two referees to benedikt.pelzer@uk-essen.de.
