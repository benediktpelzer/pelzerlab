Benedikt Pelzer, MD

Principal Investigator

Benedikt is a clinician-scientist and PI at the Department of Hematology and Stem Cell Transplantation, University Hospital Essen / West German Cancer Center. He completed postdoctoral training with Prof. Ari Melnick at Weill Cornell Medicine, New York, before returning to Germany to establish an independent research group focused on B cell lymphoma biology.
