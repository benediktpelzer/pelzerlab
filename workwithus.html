Open Positions

We are always looking for motivated MD students, PhD students, and postdocs interested in B cell lymphoma biology and translational oncology. If you are interested in joining the lab, please send your CV and a brief statement of interest to the email address above.

MD Thesis Projects

We actively support MD students pursuing a doctoral thesis in our lab. Thesis projects can be pursued through experimental laboratory work or by analyzing primary patient data from our clinical databases.

We provide active support for competitive funding applications including:

  • ELAN Programme — intramural funding for MD thesis projects at University Hospital Essen
  • DGHO Scholarships — travel and research fellowships from the German Society of Hematology and Medical Oncology
  • José Carreras Foundation — research scholarships for leukemia and lymphoma research

If you are an MD student interested in hematology and lymphoma research and would like to discuss potential thesis projects, please reach out directly.
