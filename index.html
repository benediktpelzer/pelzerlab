Understanding the transcriptional and epigenetic mechanisms that drive B cell lymphomagenesis

Our lab investigates how molecules cooperate to establish oncogenic transcriptional programs in marginal zone B cell lymphoma (MZL) as well as diffuse large B cell lymphoma (DLBCL) We combine chromatin profiling, 3D genome analysis, and preclinical models to identify novel therapeutic vulnerabilities.

Our Research Focus

We combine molecular biology, epigenomics, and clinical research to understand and target B cell lymphomas.

Extra-follicular B cell Biology

We investigate how B cell development happens outside of the germinal center reaction.

Marginal Zone Lymphomas

We are interested in the pathogenesis of MZL and how this can translated into targeted vulnerabilities.

Precision Medicine in Hematology

We translate mechanistic insights into preclinical therapeutic strategies, including small molecules and combination approaches in DLBCL.
