Bachelor & Master Students

Start your research career in lymphoma biology.

Lab Rotations

We welcome Bachelor and Master students for lab rotations. Rotations give you hands-on experience with state-of-the-art techniques in molecular biology, epigenomics, and computational analysis in the context of B cell lymphoma research.

Thesis Projects

We offer Bachelor and Master thesis projects in experimental laboratory work as well as in the analysis of primary patient data from our clinical databases. Projects are tailored to your background and interests.

How to Apply

If you are interested in a lab rotation or thesis project, please send your CV and a brief statement of interest to benedikt.pelzer@uk-essen.de.
